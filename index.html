Justice of the Peace

I am Marge Schneider, a Justice of the Peace and Spiritual Counselor. I am licensed in Connecticut but can obtain a permit for anywhere in the United States. It is my great joy and privilege to perform a memorable marriage ceremony, vow renewal, celebrate life or other uniquely special event for all individuals and loving couples. I am respectful of all faiths, no faiths, gender preferences and spiritual paths.

Create a unique and beautiful ceremony that reflects your intimate, spiritual, heartfelt emotions, personal style and relationship, values and commitment. I can provide a memorable service at your specified length or will enthusiastically collaborate and help you create what you envision.

Location

Location of choice — park, resort or the intimacy and privacy of your home or mine. My home in Tolland, Connecticut can accommodate a ceremony outdoors on our patio or in our gardens, weather permiting, or inside the house.

Food

For small, private ceremonies I can provide light, delicious snacks and beverages or an elegant, gourmet meal for an additional fee.

Music

I play the Native American flute and can incorporate it as part of your ceremony. Additionally, I can arrange for an awesome duo, Ellen & Larry of We Got Rhythm! to provide music for your celebration.

Pre-marriage Counseling

For couples who would like to deepen their emotional or spiritual connection, I will happily meet with you to discuss needs at an additional fee.
